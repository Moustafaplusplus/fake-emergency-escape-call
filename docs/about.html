Fake Emergency Escape Call

Schedule simulated incoming calls for reminders, practice, entertainment, or personal exit planning.

Important: This app schedules simulated calls only. It does not dial real phone numbers, connect to cellular networks, use VoIP to reach people, or contact emergency services (911, 112, 999, etc.).

What this app does

  • Lets you schedule a fake incoming call with a custom caller name and spoken message
  • Plays a ringtone and shows an incoming-call style screen at the scheduled time
  • Speaks your script through the phone audio path after you tap Answer (text-to-speech)
  • Stores everything locally on your device — no cloud account required in v1

What this app does not do

  • Replace your system Phone or dialer app
  • Call or message real people without your separate action
  • Guarantee delivery on all devices (battery savers and permission settings may delay alarms)
  • Provide legal, medical, or emergency advice

Responsible use

Do not use the App to impersonate law enforcement, emergency services, banks, or others in a deceptive or harmful way. You are responsible for how you use scheduled simulated calls. Misuse may violate local laws or platform policies.
