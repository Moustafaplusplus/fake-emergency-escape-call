Fake Emergency Escape Call

Schedule simulated incoming calls for reminders, practice, entertainment, or personal exit planning.

Important: This app schedules simulated calls only. It does not dial real phone numbers, use cellular voice service to reach people, operate as your system Phone app, or contact emergency services (911, 112, 999, etc.).

What this app does

  • Schedule a simulated incoming call with a custom caller name
  • Play a ringtone and show an incoming-call style screen at the scheduled time
  • After you tap Answer, play your message by text-to-speech or a voice recording you made in the app
  • Store schedules and messages locally on your device (no cloud account in v1)

What this app does not do

  • Replace your carrier dialer or place real outbound calls
  • Contact emergency services or law enforcement on your behalf
  • Guarantee alarms on every device (battery savers and denied permissions may delay rings)
  • Provide legal, medical, or safety advice

UI notice

Incoming and in-call screens are designed to resemble a phone call for simulation purposes. The App is not affiliated with your device manufacturer, carrier, or the system Phone app. You are responsible for using it honestly and lawfully.

Responsible use

Do not use the App to impersonate police, emergency services, banks, employers, or anyone else in a deceptive or harmful way. Misuse may violate local laws or platform policies.
